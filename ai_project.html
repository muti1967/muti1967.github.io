AI Emotion Detector

One of my very first projects, this program was developed by my team and I in order to achieve two primary goals. These goals included developing an AI program to be able to recognize a person's emotions, as well as gain a significant understanding as to how data science and AI development works. I was tasked with writing scripts for collecting, organizing and labeling the training data.
